Interesting facts about bears

  1. Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  2. Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.
  3. Bears care deeply about family members.
  4. Bears can smell food, cubs, a mate, or predators from miles away.

Interesting facts about pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. They can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs, and even distinguish different humans within a photograph.
  3. Pigeons and humans have in fact lived in close proximity for thousands of years.
